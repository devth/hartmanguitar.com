Bob Hartman

Bob Hartman is a guitarist and songwriter from Billings, MT with a wide range of styles and experience. His interest in guitar started at the age of 11 when he heard Chet Atkins on the radio. Playing records back at slow speeds he was able to pick out the notes, then transpose them back to the original key. Ever since then he's been pursuing and exploring many styles of guitar including fingerstyle, classical and flamenco.

Bob is married to his wife of over 30 years, Rita, and has 5 adult children. He is a Montana native and has lived in the Billings area most of his life.
